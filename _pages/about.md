Hello Everyone,
I have received PhD from Otto-von-Guericke-University Magdeburg, Germany in March 2020. Currently, I am working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Quaid-e-Awam University College of Engineering Science & Technology, Pakistan. I received my Masters from IICT Mehran UET and Aalborg University Copenhagen, Denmark in 2012. I conducted my Masters research in Aalborg University and successfully defended my thesis over there. I also received my B.E in Electronics from Faculty of Electrical, Electronics & Computer Engineering, Mehran UET, Pakistan in 2007.
My research focus is on Wireless Sensor Network, Internet of Things, Wireless Networks Industrial Internet of Things, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Emerging Communications and Networking Technologies.
